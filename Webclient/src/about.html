About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, The FlowerPlace started as a small family-run nursery in the heart of the city. With a passion for sustainable gardening and a commitment to eco-friendly practices, we've grown into a leading provider of garden supplies and landscaping services.

Our mission is to bring the joy of gardening to everyone, regardless of their skill level or the size of their outdoor space.
